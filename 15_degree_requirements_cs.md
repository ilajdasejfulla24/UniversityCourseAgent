# Computer Science Degree Requirements
Students must complete 180 ECTS credits. Core second-year courses normally include CS102, CS201, CS210, CS220, CS230, ST201, and MA201.
AI pathway students should complete CS102, ST201, MA201, AI301, then choose AI302 Deep Learning, AI303 Natural Language Processing, CS301 Algorithms, or a capstone.
Students should avoid taking more than two high-workload technical courses in the same semester unless they have strong grades and advising approval.
